01 What Is Supertext?

Supertext is a chat app. And an SMS functionality. All turned into a one of a kind communication solution used by millions of people who find our hybrid feature solves their communication problems. Either because the person they are chatting with does not have online connectivity or because they know that whatever app the receiver has - SMS always reaches them.

02 How does Supertext differ from other messaging apps?

Albeit there are many similarities between Supertext and other messaging apps, there is one big difference: our offline communication solution. Where a Whatsapp or a Telegram user needs to wait for connectivity, a Supertext user can simply send the message as an SMS via the app into the group and continue the conversation uninterrupted.

03 Is Supertext Free To Use?

Yes, Supertext offers free basic usage with premium features available.

04 How much does it cost to use Supertext?

There are no additional costs using Supertext. Like all apps, Supertext will use data on your phone. However, the service is for free if you choose to use SMS!

05 Where is Supertext Based?

Supertext started originally in Sweden but is now a geographically dispersed company with employees in Indonesia, Sweden, Moldova and Romania.

06 How does Supertext make money?

Although communication should be free, we all need to pay our bills. To do so we work with a selected number of advertisers, who we know can offer our users something they may be interested in - with attractive deals.

07 Is Supertext available in multiple languages?

Supertext is available in bahasa Indonesia, English and Swedish. More languages are being added as we move into more markets.

Supertext is available as apps for iOS and Android, as well as via SMS.

11 How do I add contacts and friends on the Supertext app?

It is easy contacting your friends via Supertext! Just allow the app to access the phonebook on your device and then you are ready! If you would like to add friends who are not in your phone book you can either do it via QR code, by adding the person’s phone number while creating a chat or a group or sharing the group using the invitation links. Remember that your friends do not need to install the app in order to join the conversation as they can simply use SMS!

23 How do I block someone from contacting me?

Android
If you do not want to receive messages or status updates from someone you have the option to block them in the app. If you are in immediate danger you should contact your local emergency services.
This happens when you block someone
1. The blocked contact will not be notified of being blocked
2. Your comments and status updates will not be visible to the blocker
3. Your blocked contact will not be able to send you messages or status updates.
4. Your blocked contact can still invite you to a group or be a member of a group that you are invited to. Please do not accept these invitations.

24 What are Supertext communities?

This feature is currently only available for Android users. Supertext communities is the space in the app where people with a common interest can join together in topic based groups. Why not create a community for your neighbourhood, your football team or for your school friends?
Any Android user can create a Supertext Community. All Communities are public.
Any Android user can join a Supertext Community.
Members of a community can comment and react to announcements, invite other people to the community and chat in community channels.

25 How do I create a community?

Anyone can create a community in Supertext. An Announcement channel will automatically be created where admins of the community can send messages that reach all members.
1. Go to the Communities tab
2. Tap on the blue “Create button”
a. Alternatively you tap on the three dots menu 3. Select “Create community”
4. Write a community name (mandatory)
5. Add a community description (optional) Your description should provide members with an idea of what your community is about.
6. You can add a community icon and cover by tapping the selecting a photo from your device’s photos or take a new one
7. Select the type of community you are creating (optional)

27 How do I delete a community?

You can delete a community by tapping the Setting button and navigating to the bottom of the screen and choosing “Delete the community”. 1. Only the administrator who created the community can delete the community.
2. If a community is deleted, all channels will also be deleted.
3. If the community has more than 200 members and you want to delete it, you need to verify yourself by following the steps shown in the app.
4. A deleted community cannot be recovered

28 What are community announcements?

Announcements in communities are a way for community admins to communicate with all members. Members can react and comment on these announcements but only admins can send them.

29 About community channels

Community admins can create channels for different topics. Community members can join these channels as they wish. A community can have many different channels and set different admins for these.

30 Community admin controls

Community admins are responsible for creating and managing their communities on Supertext. To keep their community safe for all members, administrators can:
1.Can promote or dismiss other members as admins
2. Can manage community information like name and community avatar.
3. Create and delete channels in the community.
4. Create and delete announcements
5. Remove comments from members
6. Delete the entire community. Only the admin who created the community can delete the community

31 About community admins

Only a community admin can make another community member an admin. The creator of the community cannot be removed and will remain an admin unless the community is deleted.
To assign another member as admin:
1. Go to the list of members (Settings/Manage community members).
2. Scroll or search the member you would like to make an admin
3. Select the permissions you’d like to give the person and tap confirm
4. A message goes out to the selected member

To remove another admin
Only the community creator can unassign a community admin
1. Select the admin from the list of admins 2. Tap on the user info 3. Select Unassign an admin
