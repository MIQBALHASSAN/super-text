Frequently Asked Questions

01 What Is Supertext?

Supertext is a chat app. And an SMS functionality. All turned into a one of a kind communication solution used by millions of people who find our hybrid feature solves their communication problems. Either because the person they are chatting with does not have online connectivity or because they know that whatever app the receiver has - SMS always reaches them.

02 How does Supertext differ from other messaging apps?

Albeit there are many similarities between Supertext and other messaging apps, there is one big difference: our offline communication solution. Where a Whatsapp or a Telegram user needs to wait for connectivity, a Supertext user can simply send the message as an SMS via the app into the group and continue the conversation uninterrupted.

03 Is Supertext Free To Use?

Yes, Supertext offers free basic usage with premium features available.

04 How much does it cost to use Supertext?

There are no additional costs using Supertext. Like all apps, Supertext will use data on your phone. However, the service is for free if you choose to use SMS!

05 Where is Supertext Based?

Supertext started originally in Sweden but is now a geographically dispersed company with employees in Indonesia, Sweden, Moldova and Romania.

06 How does Supertext make money?

Although communication should be free, we all need to pay our bills. To do so we work with a selected number of advertisers, who we know can offer our users something they may be interested in - with attractive deals.

07 Is Supertext available in multiple languages?

Supertext is available in bahasa Indonesia, English and Swedish. More languages are being added as we move into more markets.
