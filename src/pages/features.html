Features

Works offline and online

Do you want to have the great user interface of an app and the reach of SMS you have come to the right place! Via the Supertext app you can send messages to all of your friends - app or no app. Connected or unconnected. Write your message in the app and we will make sure it is delivered to your friend either way. For free.

Make communication easier

"Remember! Dinner at Joe’s on Friday — Pin this message at the top of your chat to make sure everyone sees it. You can also set reminders for important messages to ensure nothing gets missed!

Check out this video — Share files, photos, voice memos, and more in your messages. Easily locate shared items in your group’s settings.

Express Yourself — Use reactions to convey your feelings. Choose an emoji to express yourself quickly and funnily instead of text!"

Community space

Supertext provides structured communication for all groups and communities. Assign co-admins, post announcements with commenting functions, create channels for subtopics within the community, polling, access community statistics and more. Explore and join communities or create your own!

Unique stickers

“When words are not enough”
Say it better with stickers! Supertext gives you access to unique handcrafted stickers by local illustrators that can help you express yourself.

Public chat rooms

Do you want to chat with people with similar interests? Join a Ruang Rumpi!
This feature allows you to discuss topics with others who share the same interests. If you have the Supertext app you can choose from predefined topics and if you are using Supertext via SMS you can create your own! Each group has 15 members and a new group is created when one is full. Group members are limited to ensure chatty groups!

SuperAI personas

Meet Cinta, Willy, Kanjeng Roro, Singerti & Nek Ratih! Our AI personas are here to help or entertain you. You choose! We are adding new personas regularely.

Cinta is happy to discuss any relationship topics with you. Willy can help you with career advice. Kanjeng Roro’s strength is to look into the stars for what the future may bring and Nek Ratih is always available to give you the most horrific ghost stories!

Popular chat features

Send voice messages, videos, and images, or get creative with a fun doodle! For one-way communication, create a broadcast group where only admins receive replies. If you prefer a more interactive setting where everyone can chat together, choose chat groups instead.
